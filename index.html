Official Rules

Respect the Hive. Play Fair.

The main map is PvE. PvP is limited to approved zones, raiding follows the server schedule, and every player is responsible for understanding these rules before playing.

🌿
01

PvE World

  • The main map is PvE.
  • No killing, raiding, or damaging player bases.
  • No stealing from players.
  • Respect other players' builds and space.
  • Ask permission before building near others.
  • Do not build onto or modify another player's base.
⚔️
02

PvP Zones

  • PvP is only allowed at Monuments and the Hive Arena.
  • Enter these areas prepared for combat.
  • No safe-zone abuse or exploit tactics.
  • Loot and combat are at your own risk.
💣
03

Raiding Rules

  • Raiding is only active during Raid Weekend.
  • Raid Weekend runs from Friday 7:30 AM EST until Monday 7:30 AM EST.
  • Outside of Raid Weekend, all bases are protected.
  • Players with active Offline Protection (Zorp) remain protected.
  • Offline Protection lasts a maximum of 24 hours after logging off.
  • No exploit raiding, glitch raiding, or cheating.
🔥
04

Purge Week

  • The final 7 days before wipe are Purge Week.
  • Raiding is active 24/7 during Purge Week.
  • Expect increased PvP, raids, and competition.
  • Players with active Offline Protection (Zorp) remain protected.
  • Offline Protection lasts a maximum of 24 hours after logging off.
🛡️
05

Offline Protection

  • Offline Protection is provided through Zorp.
  • Protection only remains active for up to 24 hours after logging off.
  • Players must properly link and maintain their protection.
  • Abuse of Offline Protection systems is not allowed.
📜
06

General Rules

  • No cheating, exploiting, scripting, or glitch abuse.
  • No harassment, racism, hate speech, or excessive toxicity.
  • Respect staff decisions.
  • Keep chat free from spam.
💰
07

Trading & Economy

  • No scamming or dishonest trades.
  • Use trade channels appropriately.
  • Report issues with proof when possible.
Know the Schedule

Raid Weekend & Purge Week

Normal raiding is active only from Friday at 7:30 AM EST until Monday at 7:30 AM EST. During the final seven days before wipe, Purge Week overrides the normal schedule and raiding stays active 24/7.

Monday 7:30 AM → Friday 7:30 AM

Raiding closed. Player bases are protected.

Friday 7:30 AM → Monday 7:30 AM

Raid Weekend active. Zorp protection still applies.

Final 7 Days Before Wipe

Purge Week active with 24/7 raiding. Zorp protection still applies.

Build your Hive. Fight for your territory. Prepare for Raid Weekend. Survive the Purge.

Rules apply to everyone. Report rule-breaking with clear proof whenever possible.
